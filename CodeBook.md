Subject and Activity

These variables identify the unique subject/activity pair the variables relate to:

subject - Number indicating the subject (participant)
Activity_Label - The specific activity measured:
	- Walking
	- Walking Upstairs
	- Walking Downstairs
	- Sitting
	- Standing
	- Laying
